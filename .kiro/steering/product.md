# EnerjiOS - Solar Energy Management Platform

EnerjiOS is a comprehensive solar energy system (GES) sales and management platform built for the Turkish market. It provides end-to-end solutions for solar project design, customer management, financial analysis, and multi-role dashboards.

## Core Features

- **3D Project Designer**: Interactive solar panel placement with Google Maps integration and real-time performance calculations
- **Multi-Role System**: Admin, Company, Customer, and Farmer dashboards with role-specific functionality
- **Financial Analysis**: ROI calculations, bank comparisons, and Turkish government incentive integration
- **Agrovoltaic Systems**: Specialized agricultural solar solutions with crop compatibility analysis
- **Quote Management**: Automated quote generation and delivery system with digital signatures
- **Installation Tracking**: Complete project lifecycle management from design to completion

## Target Market

- Primary: Turkish solar energy market
- Users: Solar installers, equipment manufacturers, consultants, farmers, and end customers
- Language: Turkish (with Turkish Lira currency and regional data)
- Compliance: Turkish solar regulations and incentive programs (YEKDEM)

## Business Model

B2B2C platform connecting solar companies with customers, featuring project design tools, CRM functionality, and comprehensive project management capabilities.
